Jul 3, 2021 Neskowin Ghost Forest üì∑

While at the coast last weekend, we went on an excursion to the Neskowin Ghost Forest.

That day it was a foggy with columns of rising steam visible. I’m not sure if this is common there but it sure added to the spookiness of the name.

As in many parts of the Oregon coast there were rock formations of various sizes that dot the landscape and make it ever more interesting than just a sandy beach.
