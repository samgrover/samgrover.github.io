The way we were

Jan. 14, 2006

[![](http://static.flickr.com/38/86652958_b1a50335b4_t.jpg)](http://flickr.com/photos/samgrover/sets/72057594048405929/) My cousin sent me some scans of old photos. She is the little one in the pictures. These are from 1986/7 and were taken at our house in New Delhi. We all used to live as a joint family, ten to fifteen people spanning three generations in one house. Anyway, the photos brought back memories and I wrote about each one of them on flickr.
